--- Iteration 2 ---

Agent:
Common themes in dreams often include:

1. **Being Chased**: This is one of the most frequent dream themes, often associated with feeling threatened or being pursued by pressures in waking life.

2. **Falling**: Represents feelings of insecurity, lack of support, or a fear of losing control.

3. **Flying**: Often associated with feelings of freedom, escape, or a desire to rise above life's challenges.

4. **Being Naked in Public**: Usually signifies vulnerability, exposure, or fear of being embarrassed or judged.

5. **Losing Teeth**: Can be related to concerns about appearance, aging, or a loss of power or control.

6. **Taking an Exam**: Associated with self-evaluation, fear of failure, or feeling unprepared in waking life situations.

7. **Being Late or Missing an Important Event**: Reflects anxiety about time management, responsibility, or missing out on important opportunities.

8. **Being Lost or Trapped**: Represents feelings of confusion, lack of direction, or being stuck in a situation.

9. **Meeting a Celebrity**: Often linked to aspirations, desires for recognition, or admiration for the qualities associated with the celebrity.

10. **Death or Dying**: Not necessarily negative, it can signify an end or transformation, personal growth, or dealing with change.

These themes can have different interpretations depending on the individual's personal experiences and emotional context. Dreams are highly subjective and can vary significantly from person to person.
